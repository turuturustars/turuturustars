## ðŸ“ž Need Help?

### Common Issues:

**Q: Can't see a member in the discipline dropdown?**
A: Only active members appear. Check their status in the Members page.

**Q: Can't mark fine as paid?**
A: Fine must be unpaid (check "Unpaid" badge) and amount must be > 0.

**Q: Can't create a meeting?**
A: Make sure you fill all required fields: title, date, venue, type.

**Q: Missing a sidebar link?**
A: Refresh the page or clear browser cache.
